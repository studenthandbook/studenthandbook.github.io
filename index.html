DEVICE SETUP

Now, here is about how to set up electronic devices and get you ready for the classes in RNS. By the way, I really really recommend all of you to save your passwords onto your google account so you won't need to remember it!

SECTION 1: PORTAL

Portal is a place where you can see your schedule and all the assginments your teachers have assigned to you. It is a really important website that you will open Multiple Times A Day. That is why you should setup your portal first.


The portal's username is your firstname + your lastname firstinitial + your graduation year
For example, John Smith in Grade 10, 2020 will graduate three years later
His username should be: smithj23
The default password for the portal is Rns+theyear so in 2020 it will be Rns2020
You can change your password once you log-in into the portal and then you can see all your schedule, assignments and also dining hall menu there!

SECTION 2: EMAIL

RNS Email is another very important component for your life in RNS, there will be a lot of information sent to you by your teacher through the email. For example, your teacher may send an email to tell you where will you go to class or what the weekend activites are. This is super super important!!!


The email's username is your lastname + your firstname firstinitial
For example, John Smith's username should be smithj
The default password for the email is Rns+theyear so in 2020 it will be Rns2020
You can change your password once you log-in into the email and then click "Option" and then "Change your password".

SECTION 3: GOOGLE DRIVE

Google Drive a is very essential in your daily school life because many assignments need to be done in "Google Docs" or "Google Slides" which are similar to "Word" and "Powerpoint". Google Drive is very convinient because you can share your assignments to your teachers by entering their emails.


The email's username is your firstname.lastname@rnslearns.cc
For example, John Smith's username should be john.smith@rnslearns.cc
The default password for the email is Summer+theyear so in 2020 it will be Summer2020
You will then be prompted to change your password to a secure password.

EFFORT REPORT

Effort report is a report that comes out in October, January and April every year. It is meant to show you how much effort you put into every class. You are being evaluated based on those following things:


You can either get one of four choices in your five areas in each class. There will be "Seldom", "Occasional", "Often" and "Consistent". The best is consistent and the worst is seldom. Here is an example that what you will see in your report.


After you receive your effort report, you will need to do a 150-300 words reflection about your report. You will analyse the points you did great in the past term and also point out your insufficient parts. The effort report often comes out on a Monday and on the same Wednesday during flex-block, you will need to go to see your advisor and finish the report there.

CUMULATIVE REPORT

Cumulative report is a report that comes out in November, March and June every year. It is meant to show you how well you did in each courses. You will be given a average GPA on the right corner and then, you will have scores for each classes. There will be two scores for you in each class, one is out of 100%, the other is from 1-7.


There are also comments for each courses. If you want to improve, read them carefully and fix them in the next term.

WEDNESDAY

Wednesday is a really special day in RNS. If you are in Grade 6-10 just like me. Then you are lucky because your Wednesday will be really relaxing and fun because there are Only Two Ordinary Classes!


In this year, the first period is a health class, second period is clubs. The teacher will do presentation at the start of each month to show you some choices for clubs. You will pick three clubs that you interest the most and submit a google form like this. Then the teachers will distribute you to some reasonable clubs.


The third period on Wednesday is "Flex-Block" which means that it is a study for you to work on homeowork. If you have any questions, you are also encouraged to go and see you teacher to get some help!


Then in the afternoon, you will have two ordinary classes to finish of your day. Also, if you love playing sports, the gym will open on Wednesday after school! That is my favorite part of Wednesday because I enjoy playing basketball with my friends!
